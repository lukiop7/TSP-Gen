Detailed Description

Concrete crossover strategy class. Implements the Crossover strategy.

Two random crossover points are selected and the cities between this points of its two parents paths are swapped to get new paths (children). Cross method creates children, pick the crossover points, performs the crossover on the parents (using helper twoCross function) and returns the result of this operation.
